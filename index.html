Our Story

Our journey began as a shared vision among three IT students passionate about technology and environmental sustainability. Together, we created AQUA KEN, a game that blends learning with entertainment, designed to address one of the world’s most pressing issues: water scarcity. ​We believe that the best way to inspire action is by reaching minds early, especially the next generation.

AQUA KEN is more than just a game—it’s a movement to educate, entertain, and inspire people of all ages to care for our planet's most precious resource: water.

mission

To educate, engage, and inspire people, especially the next generation, to understand and act on water conservation through a fun, immersive gaming experience.

vision

A future where water conservation is second nature, with the next generation leading the charge towards sustainable water practices, motivated by the knowledge and awareness gained through AQUA KEN.
